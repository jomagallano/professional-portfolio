Human-Centered Intelligence

Enabling the future of work.

I believe that the greatest barrier to AI adoption isn't the technology, but the readiness of the people using it. Currently serving as a Senior Manager for AI Enablement at a global leader in market intelligence, I lead enterprise-wide adoption strategies for thousands of professionals.

As a member of the GCCC Technology Committee and an ASEAN Foundation AI Master Trainer, I operate at the intersection of global standards and local grassroots impact. My methodology is built on Human+AI Collaboration and Systems Thinking: I help organizations see the broad strategic overview of their AI future before drilling down into the specific, persona-based actions that drive day-to-day competency.
